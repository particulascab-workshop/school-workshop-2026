About the Event

We are organizing this conference at the Instituto Balseiro / Centro Atómico Bariloche, in San Carlos de Bariloche, Argentina, during the last two weeks of May 2026. The event will span approximately one and a half weeks.

The timing coincides with the 40th anniversary of the c-theorem, whose influence provides a natural historical framework for the event.

The motivation for the meeting is to bring together leading researchers and students to discuss foundational and modern methods in quantum field theory, broadly defined. Topics will include structural aspects of QFT, renormalization group flows, irreversibility, and related developments across high-energy theory and neighboring areas. The emphasis is on ideas and methods that have shaped our understanding of quantum field theory.

The scientific program is broad, aiming to highlight diverse foundational contributions and perspectives within QFT. The format will combine pedagogical lectures aimed at advanced students with research-oriented talks and informal discussions. Invited lecturers will deliver two, three, or four lectures — each lasting one to one and a half hours — on an advanced QFT topic of their choice.

The School and Workshop are partially supported by the Simons Foundation. We anticipate being able to offer support for travel and accommodation for invited lecturers.

Venue

The event will be held at the Instituto Balseiro, part of the Centro Atómico Bariloche (CAB), situated on the shores of Lago Nahuel Huapi in the foothills of the Andes, San Carlos de Bariloche, Argentina.

Address Av. Bustillo km 9.5, R8402AGP Bariloche, Río Negro, Argentina
Website www.ib.edu.ar
Access ~20 min by bus from city center (lines 10, 20); taxi or remis also available.
Airport BRC – Aeropuerto Internacional Teniente Luis Candelaria, ~15 km from IB.

Accommodation

San Carlos de Bariloche offers a wide range of accommodation options, from campus housing (available for students on a limited basis) to hotels and hostels in the city center (~10 km from IB) and along Avenida Bustillo near the campus.

Campus Housing IB Residencias

On-site student housing available on a limited basis. Priority for registered graduate students. Contact the organizers.
